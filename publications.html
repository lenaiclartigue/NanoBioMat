Selected publications and patent

Water-dispersible sugar-coated iron oxide nanoparticles

Journal: Journal of the American Chemical Society, 2011

This work reports the synthesis of iron oxide nanoparticles with different sizes, their ligand exchange toward water dispersibility, and the evaluation of their magnetic, relaxometric and magnetic hyperthermia properties.

DOI: 10.1021/ja111448t

Cooperative organization in iron oxide multicore nanoparticles

Journal: ACS Nano, 2012

This study shows how cooperative organization and magnetic coupling in iron oxide multicore nanoparticles can enhance their efficiency as heating mediators and MRI contrast agents, both in solution and in cellulo.

DOI: 10.1021/nn304477s

Nanomagnetic sensing of blood plasma protein interactions

Journal: ACS Nano, 2012

This work investigates the formation of the protein corona around iron oxide nanoparticles and its impact on macrophage uptake using a nanomagnetic sensing approach.

DOI: 10.1021/nn300060u

PEGylated anionic magnetofluorescent nanoassemblies

Journal: ACS Applied Materials & Interfaces, 2017

This article describes magnetofluorescent raspberry-like nanoassemblies and shows how their interface structure modulates MRI contrast and cellular uptake.

DOI: 10.1021/acsami.7b01737

Curcumin-based magnetic nanoassemblies

Patent: French patent FR 3 135 617 — B1, granted in 2025

This patent protects magnetic nanoassemblies based on magnetic nanoparticles, curcumin or curcumin derivatives, and stabilizing polymers, prepared by flash nanoprecipitation in a confined impinging jets mixer.
