May 2025

French patent granted for curcumin-based magnetic nanoassemblies

The French patent “Nano-assemblages à base de curcumine ou de dérivé de la curcumine et leur procédé de préparation” was granted in May 2025 by the French National Institute of Industrial Property.

This patent protects magnetic nanoassemblies based on magnetic nanoparticles, curcumin or curcumin derivatives, and stabilizing polymers, prepared by flash nanoprecipitation.

Patent number: FR 3 135 617 B1
