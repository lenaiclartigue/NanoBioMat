BioNanoMagnetics

Magnetic nanoassemblies for nanomedicine

Lénaïc Lartigue

Associate Professor in Chemistry at Nantes Université, CEISAM UMR 6230.

My research focuses on the design of magnetic iron oxide nanoparticle-based nanoassemblies for biomedical applications, including magnetic hyperthermia, controlled drug delivery and nanomedicine strategies for liver diseases.

Research topics

  • Magnetic iron oxide nanoparticles
  • Magnetic nanoassemblies
  • Magnetic hyperthermia
  • Drug delivery and immune modulation
  • Nanomedicine for liver diseases
